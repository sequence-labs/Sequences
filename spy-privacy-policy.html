Spy - Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 20, 2025

Summary (TL;DR)

  • Spy is an offline, pass‑the‑phone party game.
  • We do not collect, track, sell, or share personal data.
  • No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no internet connectivity.
  • Any game settings you add (e.g., custom words) stay on your device and are removed when you delete the app.

What We Don't Collect

  • No names, emails, phone numbers, or contact lists
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics, crash analytics, or diagnostics sent to us or third parties

Data Stored On Your Device (Locally)

  • Game configuration (e.g., player count, custom words)

This data never leaves your device and can be removed by:

  • Clearing the app's data (if available on your platform), or
  • Deleting the app

Network, Accounts, and Payments

  • The app does not require an internet connection.
  • The app does not support or require user accounts.
  • No in‑app purchases or payment processing are performed in the app.

Permissions

The app does not request sensitive permissions (e.g., camera, microphone, contacts, photos, location, Bluetooth, push notifications).

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

If you believe a child provided us with personal information, contact us and we will assist with deletion guidance (though the app itself collects none).

International Users & Legal Basis

We do not process personal data. If privacy laws (e.g., GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA) apply, our legal basis is "no processing" of personal data. Any settings remain only on your device.

Security

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, our primary security posture is minimizing data handling.

Any local settings are stored using the operating system's standard app storage.

Changes to This Policy

If we add features that change our data practices, we will update this policy and revise the "Effective date" above.
